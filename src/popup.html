How ScrollSnap works

  1. Open a webpage or document
  2. Click Start Capture
  3. Scroll naturally
  4. Stop and review
  5. Export Markdown, ZIP, or PDF
Privacy

ScrollSnap captures screenshots only after you start a session. Screenshots stay in local browser storage, are never uploaded, and you can delete sessions anytime. The extension captures only the active tab during an active session.
